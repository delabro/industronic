ABOUT INDUSTRONIC SERVICES LTD.

Industronic Services Ltd provides general Electronic and Electrical services - particularly to the Commercial and Industrial sector, and was established in 1985 by Robert Delaveau, a Technician-Engineer with over 38 years experience in the industry.

Robert started out as a Hydro-electric apprentice with the NZ Electricity Department and worked on large-scale generation plant and construction projects such as the Huntly & Marsden B power stations. Following this he has moved into the commercial world, gaining Advanced trades Certificate and also NZ Certificate of Engineering in Electronics & Computer Technology. Robert has worked on many types of equipment from CNC machines, Plastics, Mining & Petrochemical equipment, Textile machinery etc.

We support NZ companies, our customers include some of the largest & most familiar brand names in the country. We also repair equipment shipped to us from Australian companies, and beyond. For overseas OEM's we provide the local support & maintenance for your products here in NZ.
